About Jurista

At JURISTA, we go beyond traditional legal advice. We are your strategic legal partner—here to protect your ideas, power your growth, and navigate legal complexity with clarity and confidence.

Born from a vision to simplify access to high-level legal solutions, JURISTA is a forward-thinking legal consultancy based in Moldova, built by a team of dynamic, multilingual professionals. We work at the intersection of law, technology, and business, helping startups, investors, and global companies stay compliant, competitive, and ready for the future.

  • A clear focus on innovation, sustainability, and global alignment
  • Strong connections with regulators and policy-makers
  • A hands-on, business-oriented approach
  • Custom legal strategies, not templates
